Here's a traditional and flavorful Kerala-style Upma (Uppumavu) recipe — a simple breakfast dish made from roasted semolina (rava/sooji) with coconut and tempered spices, Kerala-style.

🌴 Kerala-Style Upma (Uppumavu)
📝 Ingredients (Serves 2–3)

Rava (semolina/sooji) – 1 cup (preferably coarse)
Water – 2½ to 3 cups (depending on desired softness)
Coconut (grated) – ¼ to ½ cup (fresh or frozen)
Onion (small) – 1, finely chopped
Green chilies – 2, slit
Ginger – 1 tsp, finely chopped
Curry leaves – 1 sprig
Mustard seeds – ½ tsp
Urad dal – 1 tsp (optional)
Chana dal – 1 tsp (optional)
Salt – to taste
Coconut oil – 1–2 tbsp
