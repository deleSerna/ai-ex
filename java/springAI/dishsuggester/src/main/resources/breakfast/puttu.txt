Here’s the recipe for the beloved Kerala-style Puttu – a soft, fluffy steamed rice cake made with rice flour and coconut. It's a traditional breakfast, usually served with banana, kadala curry (black chickpeas), or sugar and ghee.

🥥 Kerala Style Puttu Recipe
📝 Ingredients (Serves 2)

Puttu rice flour (roasted rice flour) – 1 cup
Water – ~½ cup (as needed)
Salt – to taste
Grated coconut – ½ cup (fresh or frozen, preferably fresh)
