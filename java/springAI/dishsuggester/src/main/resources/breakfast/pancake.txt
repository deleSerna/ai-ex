Here's a classic, fluffy Pancake recipe — super easy and great for breakfast or brunch.

🥞 Classic Pancake Recipe
📝 Ingredients (Makes 6–8 small pancakes)

All-purpose flour – 1 cup
Milk – ¾ cup to 1 cup (adjust for consistency)
Egg – 1
Sugar – 2 tbsp
Baking powder – 2 tsp
Salt – ¼ tsp
Butter (melted) – 2 tbsp (plus more for cooking)
Vanilla extract – ½ tsp (optional)
