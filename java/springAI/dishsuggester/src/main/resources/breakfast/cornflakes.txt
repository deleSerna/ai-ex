Here's a super simple and quick Cornflakes recipe — great for breakfast or a light snack.

🥣 Basic Cornflakes Recipe
📝 Ingredients (Serves 1)

Cornflakes – 1 cup
Milk (hot or cold) – ½ to ¾ cup (as preferred)
Sugar or honey – 1 to 2 tsp (optional)
Fruits (optional) – banana slices, apple cubes, berries, or dry fruits
Nuts (optional) – almonds, cashews, raisins
